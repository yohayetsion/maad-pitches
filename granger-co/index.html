01

Direction One

Refined Evolution

Elevate the existing brand with a more premium, sophisticated expression while maintaining the light, welcoming spirit that defines Granger & Co.

+ Enhanced typography with elegant serif/sans pairing
+ Richer golden accents for premium positioning
+ Photography guidelines emphasizing natural Australian light
+ Subtle brand refresh that honors existing equity
02

Direction Two

Heritage Reinvented

Place Bill Granger's extraordinary story at the heart of the brand. A celebration of the man who invented breakfast as we know it.

+ "Bill's Philosophy" integrated throughout experience
+ Archive photography and founder storytelling
+ Signature dishes with heritage narratives
+ Cookbook and content platform potential
03

Direction Three

Australian Bold

Lean fully into Australian identity with a bolder, more confident expression. Own "Australian" as a distinctive, desirable lifestyle brand.

+ Warmer, sunnier color palette with unexpected accents
+ Contemporary Australian design references
+ Stronger "At Home" retail brand extension
+ Lifestyle positioning beyond just dining
