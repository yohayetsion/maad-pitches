Your Brand Today

The Blacklock Story

From a former Soho brothel in 2015 to 7 beloved locations across the UK - you've revived the pre-war British chophouse tradition with extraordinary success.

Brand Assets
  • Heritage-inspired wordmark
  • Charcoal & slate palette with purple accent
  • "Chop Love" as emotional anchor
  • Basement speakeasy atmosphere
  • B Corp certification
What's Working
  • Distinctive positioning (chophouse vs steakhouse)
  • Founder story resonates (lawyer to restaurateur)
  • Value proposition is clear and compelling
  • Sunday roast books months in advance
  • Sharing format creates social moments
The Opportunity

As you expand regionally, your brand identity can evolve to better communicate your heritage, quality, and values - while maintaining the warmth that makes Blacklock special.

Strengths & Opportunities

Core Strengths

  • Heritage authenticity - You're not playing at tradition, you've researched and revived it
  • Value positioning - "Hawksmoor quality at accessible prices" is powerful
  • Purple differentiation - Unexpected in the steakhouse space
  • Sharing format - Creates theatre and social moments
  • People-first culture - Genuine values attract loyalty
  • Founder narrative - Gordon's story humanizes the brand

Evolution Opportunities

  • Heritage expression - Visual identity could better reflect 1690s chophouse roots
  • Purple underutilized - Your distinctive color deserves more prominence
  • Regional consistency - Manchester & Birmingham need cohesive brand application
  • Digital presence - Website feels dated vs positioning
  • Storytelling - Warren family sourcing, B Corp values underplayed
  • Typography - Could better balance heritage with modernity

Our Recommendation

Direction 2: Heritage Reinvented

With elements of Direction 1's polish

Why This Works

  • Ownable positioning - No one else can claim the chophouse heritage
  • Competitive moat - Creates clear separation from Hawksmoor and Flat Iron
  • Story-rich - Endless content opportunities around history and tradition
  • Scalable authenticity - Heritage translates to any location

The Hybrid Approach

  • Heritage depth from Direction 2's research and storytelling
  • Modern execution from Direction 1's polish and refinement
  • Purple prominence as the distinctive thread throughout
  • B Corp integration as proof of genuine values

This positions Blacklock as the definitive authority on British chop culture - authentic, accessible, and unmistakably yours.
