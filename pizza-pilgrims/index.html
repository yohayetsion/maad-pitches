Key Insight: Franco Manca just invested in their brand. Pizza Pilgrims needs to evolve to maintain differentiation.

Direction 1

"Elevated Pilgrimage"

Refined evolution - same energy, elevated execution

Keep the playful green palette and fun personality, but modernize typography, strengthen the founder story visibility, and elevate photography quality.


Evolution, not revolution.


This approach addresses the opportunities identified while preserving what makes Pizza Pilgrims distinctive. Low risk - customers know and love the brand.

Brand Elements

  • Refined green palette (deeper, richer tones)
  • Custom typography refresh
  • Hero section leads with pilgrimage story
  • Premium food photography investment
  • Streamlined CTA hierarchy

Direction 2

"Napoli Roots"

Authentic Neapolitan, contemporary expression

Lean harder into the Naples origin story. Warmer Mediterranean palette, artisanal typography, craft-forward messaging.


Position as the "real Neapolitan" vs. competitors.


Differentiates from Franco Manca's Brixton narrative. Appeals to authenticity-seeking consumers and supports premium positioning.

Brand Elements

  • Mediterranean palette (terracotta, olive, cream)
  • Hand-drawn or artisanal typography
  • Naples street photography
  • Italian language elements
  • Craft and tradition messaging

Direction 3

"Community Kitchen"

Your neighborhood pizza pilgrimage

Emphasize the community aspect - local pizzerias, masterclasses, charity work, staff ("pizza pilgrims"). Each location as a community hub.


Warmer, more inviting, less chain-feeling.


Leverages B Corp status and charity partnerships. Addresses the risk of 25+ locations feeling corporate. Supports events and experiences positioning.

Brand Elements

  • Warm earth tones with green accents
  • Location-specific content & stories
  • Staff and community photography
  • Handwritten/personal design touches
  • Local partnership highlights
Our Recommendation

Direction 1 + Elements of Direction 2

Elevate the pilgrimage story while strengthening Neapolitan credentials. Evolution that protects what works while addressing what doesn't.
